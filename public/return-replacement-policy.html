Return & Replacement

Return & Replacement Policy

At Drop2Wave, we are committed to ensuring a smooth and transparent shopping experience. Please read our return and replacement policy carefully before placing an order.

Return Policy

  • You can return a product only under the following conditions:
  • The product must be checked immediately in front of the delivery agent.
  • If you receive a wrong, defective, or damaged item, you can return it on the spot.
  • If you do not like the product, you must return it instantly in front of the delivery agent by paying the delivery charge.
  • Once the delivery agent leaves, return is not applicable. However, you may contact us, and we will assist you with a possible exchange.

Replacement Policy

Replacement is applicable within 3 / 5 / 7 days (depending on the product category).

  • Applicable only for electronic products
  • No replacement for non-electronic items
  • Not applicable for change of mind
  • Product must be unused
  • Damaged due to misuse or customer negligence will not be eligible
  • Must include original packaging
  • Applicable only for wrong / defective / damaged items

Refund Policy

  • We do not offer refunds.
  • In special cases, products will be exchanged instead.
  • If the replacement product has an issue, we may offer a better-quality product.
  • In such cases, a small additional charge may apply.

Return Shipping Cost

Return shipping cost will be shared:

  • 50% by Customer
  • 50% by Drop2Live

Important Instructions

Always check the product in front of the delivery agent.

If possible, record an unboxing video as proof (clear and complete).
